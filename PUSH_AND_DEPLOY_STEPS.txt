═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
WHY THIS WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

When you run `vercel --prod` from a directory, Vercel treats THAT
directory as the project root. 

- From root: Vercel looks for package.json in root (doesn't exist)
- From frontend: Vercel looks for package.json in frontend (exists!)
